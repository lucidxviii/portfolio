PaCO₂35 – 45mmHgRespiratory component; ↑ = hypoventilation, ↓ = hyperventilation

PaO₂80 – 100mmHg<80 = hypoxemia; <60 = respiratory failure threshold
SaO₂95 – 100%<90% = significant hypoxemia requiring intervention
